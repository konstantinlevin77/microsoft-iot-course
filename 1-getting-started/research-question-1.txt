Do a little research: How much of the data generated by IoT devices is actually used, and how much is wasted? Why is so much data ignored?

According to Gartner, (2018, as cited in GoodData, 2019) an American technology research and consulting firm, %80 of the data generated by IoT devices are wasted as companies working in the industry right now is mainly focused on collecting data rather than effectively processing it.
